MojAbble Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

What we collect

MojAbble collects the player name you enter (up to 16 characters) and your game scores. This information is sent to our server for the global leaderboard. No account or sign-up is required.

How we use it

Player names and scores are used solely to display the global leaderboard within the app. We do not use this data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Multiplayer

When you create or join a multiplayer room, your score and game progress are shared with your opponent in real time. Room data is automatically deleted after 30 minutes.

Data stored on your device

Your player name, local high scores, and rarest words are saved in your browser's local storage on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you submit a score to the global leaderboard.

Data stored on our server

The global leaderboard stores the top 50 scores and top 50 rarest words. Each entry includes the player name, score, difficulty, and word count. IP addresses are temporarily stored for rate limiting and are not logged permanently.

Third-party services

MojAbble uses the Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) to fetch word definitions. This request includes the word you submitted but no personal information. We do not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

MojAbble does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The only data collected is a self-chosen player name and game scores.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
