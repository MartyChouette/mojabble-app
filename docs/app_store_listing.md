## Description

MojAbble is a word game that combines mahjong tile mechanics with competitive word building. Pick free tiles from a layered board, spell words, and chain combos for massive scores.

Features:
- Layered tile board inspired by mahjong solitaire. Only exposed tiles are playable.
- 196,000+ word dictionary (NWL2023 / Official North American Scrabble Dictionary).
- Three difficulty modes: Easy, Normal, Hard. Letter distributions and vowel ratios shift with each.
- Combo system. Submit words back-to-back without mistakes to build score multipliers.
- Shuffle and Swap let you rearrange the board at a point cost.
- Word stats toast shows definition, rarity, and score breakdown for every word you play.
- Global and local leaderboards. Track your high scores and rarest words found.
- Head-to-head multiplayer. Create or join a room, both players get the same seeded board, race to the highest score.
- Works offline for single player.
